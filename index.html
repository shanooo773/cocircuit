About CoCircuit Power Consultants

An independent engineering partner, built around how you actually work

CoCircuit Power Consultants is a newly established engineering and technical consulting practice, built around a simple idea: power systems engineering shouldn't be a black box. We work in partnership with our clients rather than as an outside vendor — helping de-risk technical decisions and turning complex analysis into clear, actionable guidance. We work as an extension of your team, offering direct access to senior engineers rather than layers of account management.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to make power systems engineering simpler and less risky for the people who depend on it. We work in partnership with our clients, not as an outside vendor, to de-risk technical decisions, simplify the compliance process and help our clients focus on what they do best.

Our Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to support the shift to a decarbonised, more reliable power system — helping renewable energy developers connect efficiently, and helping industrial and infrastructure operators understand and modernise the electrical systems they depend on.
