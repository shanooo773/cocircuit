Summary

Over twenty years advancing high voltage engineering education and research

With over twenty years of experience in higher education, Majid currently contributes as an Assistant Professor at Heriot-Watt University. Leveraging expertise in research and teaching, particularly in the field of high voltage electrical engineering, he fosters academic excellence and innovation while committing to advancing knowledge and supporting impactful education. Certified as a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA) and a Chartered Engineer (CEng), Majid founded CoCircuit Power Consultants to bring that same rigour and clarity to industry-facing engineering consultancy — empowering clients through collaborative, research-backed technical guidance.
